## Kerrick's General Thoughts

I feel like I was harsh to the group today, I think it's the fact that I never thought I would see us split like this, especially Adam. It's seems as though all of us have taken on different burdens, but we're scared to lean on one another for stability. . 

## Kerrick's Thoughts on the group

### [[Tarvo|Adam]]

You've taken on the burden of abandonment, your Father pushed you, your Mother did the same for safety, yet both had the same effect. Loneliness. 

Maybe you've made these mistakes because I haven't put any trust into you since the start. I've done just as they did, and for that, I apologize.

You're a man, both You and Tarvo, and you deserve to be treated as such. I could see Faust treating you as such, maybe that's why you chose to have this meeting with him in the first place. 

As soon as we put our trust into each other, you told us the entire plan Faust gave you. 

We're you investigate Florien and Faust? If so, perhaps I have been ignoring just how brilliant you truly are. 

### [[Florien|Florien]]

It seems as though you've taken on the burden of pressure. 

It appears you've made yourself not only a target of the city, but now the group. 

Why would you choose to become him? 

Did he threaten you?

Maybe, he threatened Rona...

I don't know what you're intentions are, what plans you made behind closed doors but regardless, I feel as though if we knew what your plans are, what Faust said to you, we could help you in more ways than you would think.

I will make one thing clear, Wolfrics. 

*Do not mistake us as prey*

### [[Rona]]

It took me some time to see it, but it appears you've taken on the burden of Neglect.

You fight hard for those around you, and you fight even harder to keep them around. You've seen much hardship in the past haven't you? 

I fear what you went through to lead to not only so much care in the world, but caring how much it views you as well. 

A bard fits you well, you will not be ignored. 

I fear for one thing though, with the want of being seen, comes the possibility of being manipulated by those who do inevitably see you. 

If you want me, or any of us to assist you in this plan of yours in anyway, we must discuss this plan of Yasin's, with Yasin himself. 

If I, or anyone is to protect you, I must know what you're walking into, and he's proven the only person who truly knows the plan, is himself.
