### Varna

_"A land of shadows and whispered rebellion."_

Varna is a forsaken realm tucked behind treacherous mountains and veiled in unnatural mists. Few from the Empire have crossed its borders and returned, and those who have speak in half-mad ramblings of ancient magic and wandering heretics. The region is riddled with dangerous enchantments and outlandish beliefs—none more absurd than the myth of “Dragon Riders” who protect those deemed “Pure of Heart.” These are childish tales meant to inspire rebellion and foster hope among the misguided.

Rumors persist that Varna shelters insurgents and traitors to the Empire, resisting the benevolent rule of Aerys. Though the Emperor’s light has not yet graced Varna, when it does, it will bring order to this wild and corrupted land.


### Roh

_"A wasteland that tests the strength and patience of the Empire."_

Roh is a blasted wasteland of endless dunes and burning skies—a barren stretch of hardship that serves as a natural barrier between the Empire and the lawless fringes of Wyrda. Its blistering sands and endless dunes swallow travelers whole, and even the hardiest souls find little comfort there. Water is a luxury, and the inhabitants—hardened and desperate—are often more dangerous than the unforgiving landscape.

The Empire, in its infinite grace, has extended aid to the people of Roh time and again. Yet, stubborn pride and misplaced suspicion often reject the guiding hand that seeks to lift them from destitution. It is said the desert itself was shaped by ancient magic, a buffer of divine providence meant to protect the Empire from the corruption that festers beyond its borders..

### Kaerwin

_"The realm of trials and divine punishment."_

Kaerwin is spoken of in reverence and fear, a land far beyond the reach of most mortals. Those who seek this distant region must first endure the harshness of Roh, and few emerge unscathed. The stories of this place strain credulity: colossal temples that reach the heavens, sacred springs said to grant immortality, and ancient rites that predate even the founding of Aerys.

Divine magic is said to course through the land, testing the faithful and punishing the unworthy.

No verifiable records exist of Kaerwin’s returnees, and thus it is widely regarded as a cursed land. Its claims of ancient power are little more than blasphemous boasts. The Empire, eternal and unwavering, shall one day reclaim these lost secrets and bring Kaerwin to heel.

### Mirehall

_"A quiet, sturdy province that owes its success to the Empire."_

Mirehall is a stable, if unremarkable, region of thick forests and rocky coasts. Its people are hardworking, producing ample timber and seafood that support the Empire’s growing cities. The region’s mountainous terrain is challenging but not insurmountable, and its towns are bastions of order and tranquility.

Some idle gossips suggest that the first Emperor himself was born in Mirehall, though such claims are fanciful at best. The true heart of the Empire is and will always be Aerys. Still, Mirehall is a loyal and productive province—a shining example of what the Emperor’s light can achieve.

### Skarn

_"A treacherous coast of storms and scoundrels."_

Skarn is a windswept coastal region where the rains fall hard and often, turning the land into a mire of mud and rock. Farming is a constant struggle, and the sea’s bounty is the region’s lifeline. However, where the Empire’s watchful eye falters, smugglers and bandits thrive. Hidden coves and shadowy inlets have become havens for criminals seeking to evade imperial justice.

Despite these challenges, the Emperor’s will is strong, and efforts to restore order in Skarn continue. It is only a matter of time before the region is fully pacified, and its lawless elements brought to heel.

### Ashvara 

_"A shattered realm where fools go to die."_

Ashvara is a fractured land of floating islands and crumbling ruins, a testament to the folly of those who came before the Empire. Its terrain is as dangerous as its history—jagged cliffs, shifting earth, and ancient relics that promise power but deliver death.

Those who enter Ashvara do so at great peril, lured by tales of hidden treasures and forgotten magic. Most find only their graves. Yet even this wild frontier will one day know the Emperor’s grace, when it is tamed and brought into the fold of civilization.

### Tharos

_"A land of fire and rebellion, soon to be extinguished."_

At the southernmost edge of Wyrda lies Tharos, a volcanic region of smoke and ash. Its people are stubborn and rebellious, clinging to old grudges and rejecting the Empire’s benevolence. Their so-called “Emperor” is a pretender—a petty tyrant issuing demands he has no right to make.

The Empire has been patient with Tharos, but its patience is not without limit. Soon, the fires of rebellion will be quenched, and Tharos will be reforged in the image of Aerys.

### Myrcath

_"A cursed wasteland, awaiting salvation."_

Myrcath is a nightmare given form—a land of shifting landscapes and untamed wilds where monsters roam freely. Its terrain is a chaotic jumble of deserts, forests, mountains, and volcanic craters, as if the gods themselves abandoned it in disgust. The region is officially declared uninhabitable, yet the beasts that dwell within it thrive, growing stronger with each passing year.

The Emperor’s light will shine on Myrcath in time, purging it of its horrors and restoring it to its rightful place within the Empire’s dominion. Until then, it remains a grim reminder of the wilds that lie beyond civilization.


### Iskar

_"A land rich in resources and loyal to the crown."_

Iskar is a land of ancient bones and whispered legends. Its winds howl through forgotten ruins, carrying with them the echoes of a bygone age. The Empire’s banners fly proudly here, a testament to the victories of old. It was here that the First Emperor defeated the last of the Dragonkind, bringing peace and order to a chaotic world.

Its fertile land and ample mineral deposits make it a cornerstone of the Empire’s economy. Unlike the wilder regions of Wyrda, Iskar remains firmly under the control of Aerys, its people enjoying the peace and prosperity that only the Empire can provide.

It is a testament to the Emperor’s wisdom and the enduring strength of the Empire.


### Eryndell

_"A beacon of prosperity and proof of the Empire’s benevolence."_

Eryndell stands as a testament to the Empire’s civilizing hand—a land of golden fields, lush vineyards, and bustling coastal villages. The gentle hills and temperate climate have made it the breadbasket of Aerys, with its fertile soil yielding abundant crops year after year. From its well-maintained roads to its thriving markets, every corner of Eryndell reflects the stability and order that only the Empire can offer.

Bordering the wilds of Ashvara, Eryndell is the last bastion of civilization before the chaotic frontier. Imperial patrols keep the region safe from any lingering threats, ensuring that its people sleep soundly beneath the watchful gaze of Aerys. Some whisper of ancient ruins hidden deep in its forests, remnants of forgotten ages best left undisturbed.

But such rumors are of no concern to the hardworking folk of Eryndell. Their future is bright, illuminated by the light of the Empire. Here, the promise of peace and prosperity is not a dream—it is a way of life.
