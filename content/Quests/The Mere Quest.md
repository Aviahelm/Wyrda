## The Mere Quest

**The tavern stinks of stale ale, damp wood, and too many unwashed bodies pressed together. The air is thick with laughter and murmured deals, but one voice cuts through it all—sharp, unimpressed, and unmistakably bored.**

_"So you all made it back. Huh, I guess I aught to cancel the rescue team"_ [[Yehnora]] leans lazily against the bar, wiping out a tankard with an old rag that might be making it dirtier. She eyes the party, lips curled into a smirk, like she’s already taken the measure of them and found them lacking.

_"Wait, you don't actually believe that I sent a rescue team right? You're all not that dumb."_ She says with a grin as she winks at the group before noticing the torn piece of paper as you all put it on the counter. _"So you picked [[Mere]]?"_

She sets the tankard down with a dull thud and props her elbow on the counter, chin resting on her palm. _"Listen, I don't know what stroke of dumb luck got you this far, but if you’re hoping to find someone in Mere and come back breathing, you’re either **very** brave or **very** stupid. My gold’s on the latter."_

She glances around the tavern, lowering her voice—but not by much. _"The guy you're looking for? Bad idea. People don’t just ‘go missing’ in Mere. They get **taken, buried, or regrettably digested**—in that order. And if he’s been gone more than a day, chances are, he’s already part of the scenery."_

Her eyes flicker over the group again, gauging their reaction. With a theatrical sigh, she straightens, finally pushing the crumpled scrap of parchment toward them.

_"Fine. Since you're so eager to get yourselves killed, here’s where he was last seen—a run-down shack near the river, not far from the old sawmill. If you find him, I’ll be shocked. If you find him **alive**, I might actually start believing in miracles."_

She gives them an exaggerated grin before turning away to fill another mug, throwing one last remark over her shoulder.

_"Just try not to make a mess on the way out, yeah? I’d hate to have to clean up after another batch of overconfident corpses."_
