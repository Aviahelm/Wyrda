| Luther | Human   | He/Him   | Alive  |

### Occupation:

Cleric with the Dracor Order.

### Location:

Apartments - Waterdeep

### Appearance:

Luther is a tall, muscular man, typically dressed in all white. His tattoos, inscribed across his scalp, are visible, while marks on his hands are usually concealed beneath the long sleeves of his shirt. He appears to be on a break from duty, as indicated by the stubble beginning to grow on his face.

### Mannerisms: 

A man of few words, Luther relies on his presence and stature to make an impression. He moves with purpose and efficiency, appearing uneasy when idle. There is a subtle sense that he is keeping something hidden, though he rarely reveals more than what is necessary.

### Allegiance: 

Dracor Order

### Public Perception:

He is reclusive and very little people know of his presence in general. 

Within his order, he is well regarded and appreciated as a hard worker.

### Relationships:

[[Tarvo|Adam:]] Slightly Positive

He finds Adam interesting. Chance has brought Adam to Luther and he seems to think this means more than what appears to be. Especially, due to the blockage Adam is facing.
