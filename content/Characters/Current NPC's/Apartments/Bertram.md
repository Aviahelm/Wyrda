| NAME    | SPECIES | PRONOUNS | STATUS |

| Bertram | Tabaxi  | He/Him   | Alive  |

### Occupation:

Manager of the Nirpana Orphanage

### Location:

Apartments - Waterdeep

### Appearance:

Bertram is a well-dressed man, typically seen in a white long-sleeved collared shirt and black trousers. A faint scar marks his left ear, and he notably goes without shoes. His Tabaxi features are pronounced and expressive, something he displays proudly to put the children under his care at ease.

### Mannerisms: 

He speaks in a soft, posh British accent, his words deliberate and unhurried. His voice often dips into a near whisper, lending his presence a calm and patient air.

### Allegiance: 

To the children of the Nirphana Orphanage

### Public Perception:

Well-meaning and patient, Bertram is generally respected for his kindness and composure. However, his Tabaxi heritage draws quiet prejudice from certain circles, and he is occasionally looked down upon despite his good intentions.

### Relationships:
