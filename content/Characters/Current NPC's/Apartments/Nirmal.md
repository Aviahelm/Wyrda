| Nirmal | Half-Elf | He/Him   | Alive  |

### Occupation:

Hunter and Business Owner

### Location:

Apartments - Waterdeep

### Appearance:

Nirmal has long blonde hair and wears a shark tooth necklace. His red robe fits closely, highlighting his physique, and his body is marked with several tattoos across his arms, ribs, and back. He carries himself with pride in his appearance, projecting a sense of toughness that suggests he wishes to appear more imposing than he truly is.

### Mannerisms: 

Nirmal carries himself with a calculated sense of toughness, often deepening his voice to sound more commanding, especially when caught off guard. He presents the air of a skilled hunter, moving and speaking as though he is accustomed to danger, yet there are subtle signs that much of this is performative. He favors those who display strength and openly shows disdain for perceived weakness, while his pride in his appearance and tattoos suggests that image is as important to him as any real skill or experience.

### Allegiance: 

Himself

### Public Perception:

Nirmal is often noticed for his striking appearance and confident demeanor. Many regard him as someone who enjoys the trappings of danger and adventure, though some quietly suspect that his persona is more style than substance. He is seen as bold and proud, yet occasionally theatrical in his mannerisms.
