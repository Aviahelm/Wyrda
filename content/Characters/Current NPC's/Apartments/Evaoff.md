### Occupation:

Librarian of the Second Waterdeep Library

### Location:

The Route to the Apartments - Waterdeep

### Appearance:

Evaoff has striking elven features, though they are marred by dark circles under his eyes, stubble, and a perpetually tired expression, giving the impression of little sleep. He wears comfortable, casual clothing that contrasts with the elegant surroundings of the library he inhabits.

### Mannerisms: 

Evaoff moves with minimal effort, appearing indifferent to those around him. He frequently uses telekinesis for various tasks, handling objects with ease while showing little concern for people. His focus is clearly on the books and knowledge he surrounds himself with rather than on social interaction.

### Allegiance: 

Himself

### Public Perception:

Evaoff is generally regarded with indifference by most. Patrons and visitors notice his striking elven features and odd mannerisms, but few feel strongly about him. He is seen primarily as someone devoted to his books, with little concern for social niceties or the people around him.
