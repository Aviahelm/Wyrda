### Occupation:

### Location:

### Appearance:

Zeffo is a Tabaxi who favors comfortable clothing, typically a baggy t-shirt and cargo pants with a space cut for their tail. They attempt to appear more professional by wearing glasses without lenses, giving the impression of seriousness.

### Mannerisms: 

Zeffo is often absorbed in their own thoughts, focusing intently on whatever is immediately in front of them. They can get easily overexcited, especially when discussing topics related to their work as a fanfiction writer. Their energy is lively but occasionally scattered, giving them a spacey and enthusiastic presence.

### Allegiance: 

Themselves

### Public Perception:

Zeffo is generally well liked for their enthusiasm and creativity. However, their tendency to become overly absorbed in their own thoughts and focus on small details can make them difficult to converse with, giving some the impression that they are scattered or hard to follow.
