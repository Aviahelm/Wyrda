### Occupation:

Former Royal Nanny to [[Tarvo]]

### Location:

Apartments - Waterdeep

### Appearance:

Once known for her raven-black hair and graceful frame, Hypatia’s beauty has since faded into subtlety. Now in her fifties, her hair has turned grey and her figure has grown thin. She keeps herself wrapped in a shawl, hiding what time has taken and wisdom has replaced.

### Mannerisms: 

In her youth, Hypatia carried herself with quiet confidence, her speech practiced and formal. A royal tone meant to instill discipline and dignity in young Adam. Now she speaks softly, her voice unsteady from disuse. Around others, she appears cautious, moving with the hesitance of someone long removed from comfort.

### Allegiance: 

Aerys.

### Public Perception:

She's considered a harmless recluse due to how little she interacts with others. 

Those who remember her speak highly of her remembering a woman of grace and composure.

### Relationships:
