### Appearance:

Romeo is short in stature, something he strongly resents being commented on by anyone except his wife. A white Harengon, he typically wears a purple jacket and carries a brown satchel and journal.

### Mannerisms: 

In another life, Romeo might have been a renowned monk. Instead, he is known as an accomplished artist, moving with speed and dexterity and capable of playing multiple instruments at once. He is curious and kind, though quick to rant if described as “cute” or “fluffy.”

Romeo Shade is one of the more in demand upcoming artists due to his exploration of greater Wyrda with stories of different regions, species and all sorts of interesting stories. 

He's known as quite the romantic as his wife Juliet is also an explorer and is currently in Iskar.
