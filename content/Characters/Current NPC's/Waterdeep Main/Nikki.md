### Appearance:

Nikki wears a sparkling blonde sequin dress and diamond earrings, carefully chosen to draw attention and reflect her sense of style. Her hair is a bright golden blonde, likely dyed to enhance its sheen, and she is widely regarded as attractive. She carries herself with the confidence of someone accustomed to being noticed.

### Mannerisms: 

Nikki is direct and unflinching, often coming across as rude or disrespectful. She does not mince words, speaking with brutal honesty, yet her knowledge and experience are evident. Her frankness can serve a practical purpose, helping travelers navigate Upper Waterdeep and avoid missteps that might mark them as outsiders or of lower status.

### Allegiance:

Rulha Branch in Waterdeep.

### Public Perception:

Though many view Nikki as rude or disrespectful, her striking appearance and polished presence often soften the impact of her words. Some observers find themselves defending her, whether out of admiration or the impression that her confidence and style warrant leniency.
