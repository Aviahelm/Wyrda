### Location:

Mostly roams around Waterdeep's Entrance.

### Appearance:

Aiko presents as an older woman, her hair white and neatly kept. She is always seen wrapped in a thick scarf and layered jacket, shielding herself from the cold. The clothing conceals much of her form with a small limp as she moves from place to place.

### Mannerisms: 

Aiko speaks as if she's been here a long time. Her voice quiet and soft as she often pauses before responding. There is a subtle sense that she notices more than she lets on.

### Allegiance: 

Herself

### Public Perception:

Most of Waterdeep sees Aiko as an older woman who keeps to herself. She is often observed staring into the distance, appearing lost in thought, and seems largely focused on her own business, drawing little attention beyond her quiet presence.
