| Bryson | Halfling | He/Him   | Alive  |

### Occupation:

Guild Teller at the Guild of Waterdeep

### Location:

The Route to Lower Waterdeep

### Appearance:

Bryson is a Halfling of small stature with dull brown hair and blue eyes. His skin is pale and dark circles under his eyes hint at long hours and exhaustion. He moves with a slight lethargy, as if weighed down by fatigue. His hands are callused. 

### Mannerisms: 

Moves and speaks very slowly, often pausing mid-sentence as if recalling details. Forgetful at times, but deliberate in thought. Though he appears lethargic, he is competent and careful with his duties, showing sharp understanding when given time.

### Allegiance: 

The Guild of Waterdeep

### Public Perception:

Well-known among guild staff and frequent clients; respected for his diligence despite his slow pace. For newcomers, he's a nightmare especially since he takes quite a bit of time and is forgetful.
