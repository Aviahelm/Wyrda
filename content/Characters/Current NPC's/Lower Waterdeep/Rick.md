### Appearance:

Rick is a tall muscular man. His voice doesn't fit his voice and he's gotten a shaved head apart from a strip of hair on top which is tied into a ponytail. 

He has a long beard and looks quite scary. 

### Mannerisms: 

Despite his intimidating look, Rick is gentle and soft-spoken once approached. He is easily excitable, especially when discussing fitness or training, and displays an almost boyish enthusiasm in those moments. When conversations turn embarrassing or personal, he becomes visibly flustered, often turning red and avoiding eye contact.

### Allegiance: 

1. Rick's Gym
2. Evie

### Public Perception:

Among his gym members, he is widely admired and respected for his dedication and results. To the wider public, however, his intense demeanor and imposing appearance make him seem intimidating. He is generally known as highly business-minded, prioritizing efficiency and professionalism over approachability.
