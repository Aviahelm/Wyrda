| Betty | Human   | She/Her  | Alive  |

### Occupation:

Baker at Betty's Bouncing Eatery

### Location:

Lower Waterdeep

### Appearance:

Betty is a curvy woman with a neat bob haircut and a single prosthetic leg that she moves on with practiced ease. She has several small scars across her hands that hint at long hours in a busy kitchen. A curious tattoo rests along her neck, although she seldom explains it. Confident in her craft, she often declares herself one of the best chefs in Waterdeep.

### Mannerisms: 

Betty is very talkative and carries herself with the confidence of a former fighter. She moves with the awareness of a mercenary and often keeps her eyes scanning her surroundings. Despite her bold presence, she becomes flustered when complimented and usually goes red and stutters for a moment before recovering her composure.

### Allegiance: 

The Rebels of Waterdeep

### Public Perception:

She's considered one of the better chefs in Lower Waterdeep. She often gives food away to people who can't afford it and she's well liked. Although she is also someone feared due to her past.
