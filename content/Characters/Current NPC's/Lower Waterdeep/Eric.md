### Appearance:

Eric has dark circles under their eyes and carries multiple piercings on their nose, eyebrow, and ears. Tattoos run from their neck down across their body, marking their artistic expression. They often wear new outfits, showcasing their personal style and creative flair.

Their hands move expressively while speaking, as if performing a dance, and they rarely stand still, often shifting or leaping with bursts of enthusiasm.
