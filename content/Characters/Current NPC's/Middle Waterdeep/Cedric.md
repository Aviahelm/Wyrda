| Cedric | Gnome   | He/Him   | Alive  |

### Occupation:

Translator

### Location:

Middle Waterdeep

### Appearance:

Cedric is a short gnome with pale grey skin and black hair, features they often try to conceal with varying success. He wears glasses and typically dresses in a white dress shirt paired with grey pants, presenting a neat, understated appearance.

### Mannerisms: 

Cedric is known to be picky and quite finnicky with whom and what he chooses to do business with. He tends to be on the more bored side as a majority of what he is asked to do are letters from other towns. 

### Allegiance: 

Himself

### Public Perception:

Cedric is regarded as one of the great translators, capable of understanding most languages with ease. He selectively chooses his work, only taking assignments that are well-paid or intellectually stimulating, which has earned him a clientele primarily among the wealthy and influential.
