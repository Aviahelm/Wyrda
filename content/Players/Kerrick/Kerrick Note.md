**To Kerrick Lurgan,**

Encoded for your eyes alone.

May the Light of the Empire illuminate your path, Sir.

It has come to our attention that you have entered **Mere**—a festering wound on the coastline of Skarn. While we do not presume to question your intentions, it is difficult to understand why one of your stature, the **Dragon Slayer of the West**, would waste his time in such a backwater cesspit. Mere is beneath you, and yet, here you are.

For your safety, and to ensure the Empire does not lose a valued **asset**, allow me to enlighten you about the rot you now tread upon.

Mere is divided into **four distinct regions**, each infested with its own breed of criminal filth.

- **The Entrance**: You will encounter unsettling displays—cages of humans, elves, and other creatures, intended to deter the weak-willed. Do not be deceived by its theatrics; it is a neutral ground where all factions operate under a fragile truce.
    
- **The Black Roses**: Information brokers and smugglers of unknown depth. They possess **significant wealth**, yet we cannot ascertain how they maintain it. The Empire does not take kindly to undeclared fortunes, and should you uncover such things, it would be in your best interest to report them.
    
- **The Black Axes**: Vile traffickers of sentient beings, defiling our great Empire with their hidden operations. Should you find their dens, I trust you will **cleanse** them accordingly.
    
- **The Dishonored**: A shadowy faction about which even our intelligence is uncertain. Assassins, most likely. Or worse. Keep your guard raised.
    
- **The Unnamed Zone**: A mystery even to us. No entrance, no clear rulers. Whatever lies within, it is hidden well.
    

Tread carefully, Kerrick Lurgan. The Empire has invested much in you, and it would be unfortunate if your loyalty—or your life—were called into question over a place so insignificant.

Remember who you serve.

**For the Empire.**
