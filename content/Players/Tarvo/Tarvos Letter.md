**My dearest Adam,**

It breaks my heart to write these words, but I must, for your sake. There are shadows closing in around us—whispers of treachery I can no longer ignore. I have learned that the Emperor’s second wife has laid plans against you, and though it pains me beyond words, I must take you far away until the danger has passed.

The nobles have turned against me. They see my common birth as a stain on the court, something unworthy of a prince like you. They believe the second prince can replace you as easily as a pawn on a chessboard. I write this so that one day, when you are older, you might understand why I made these choices. Every step I take is to protect you, even when it feels like I am tearing our lives apart.

If Kerrick were here, I would feel braver, stronger. His presence has always been a light in dark times. But with the Emperor sending him away, I feel the weight of this world pressing down on me, and I am frightened by what the days ahead might hold. I have made arrangements for us to hide in Mere until Kerrick returns. From there, we will travel to Waterdeep, where I pray we will be safe.

You are so young now, too young to understand the dangers that swirl around you like a storm. But one day, you will see that every choice I made was for you. You are my heart, Adam—every beat of it belongs to you.

Only Hypatia, my most trusted maid, and Gordon, the butler, know of our plans. I trust them with my life… and with yours.

I pray every night that this letter will be nothing more than a keepsake from a mother who worried too much. But if it isn’t, if the road ahead is more difficult than I imagine, hold fast to these words: You are brave. You are strong. You are loved beyond measure.

May the gods protect you, my precious boy.

With all my love,  
**Mother**
