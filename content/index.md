# Welcome to Wyrda. 

Hello! My name is Aviahelm, and this is my D&D Campaign: Wyrda. I'm responsible for *'crafting the narrative and the world'* drawing inspiration from [[How we do D&D| so many lovely creators]] and official sourcebooks. 
## A little brief. 

This Wyrda campaign started *around February 2025* as a one-shot named [[The Revellia Quest]]. The players loved it (and so did I, as a new DM), so we decided to continue the adventure and expand the setting. 

The story has taken the party from the whistling mountains of Revellia to the dangerous coastal city of [[Mere]] to the now famous city of [[Waterdeep]], where the adventure is currently unfolding.

This website serves as the official lore compendium and will consistently be updated with details, histories, notes easily searchable as well as our characters thoughts based on the session that had just occurred.
