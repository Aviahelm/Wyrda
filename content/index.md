## What is this ?

Well... That's a great question. 

The answer to that question is a bit complex.

This is kind of written as a lore depo for my d&d campaign. This will be updated to add #NPC #Player #Lore #World and all other sorts of things I find interesting. 

## Oh so this is for D&D ?

Yep! So we started with a one-shot of the story of [[Revellia]]. 

The #Player's had fun and so did I and wanted to continue in this world and expand on their own characters. I like story building and as a result this came about.
